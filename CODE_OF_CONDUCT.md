# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to provide a respectful, harassment-free environment for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Focus discussion on technical merit.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Trolling or repeated bad-faith behavior.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and may remove or reject contributions that violate this code.
